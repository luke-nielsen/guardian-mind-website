Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 22, 2024

At Screen Pigeon, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you use our website, products, and services ("Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our Services:

  • Personal Information: Information you provide when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact customer support, such as your name, email address, billing address, and payment information.
  • Device Information: Information about the laptop you use, including hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Information about your use of our website and software, such as page views, features used, and software interactions.
  • Screenshot Accountability Data: The screenshot accountability software may collect metadata (e.g., timestamp, application in use) related to screenshots for transparency purposes. The content of screenshots is not stored or transmitted to Screen Pigeon.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and Improve Services: Fulfill orders, deliver products, and enhance the functionality of our software.
  • Communicate with You: Respond to your inquiries, send order confirmations, updates, and promotional content (with your consent).
  • Ensure Security and Compliance: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Analyze Usage: Monitor and analyze trends and usage to improve our website, software, and overall user experience.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following ways:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, shipping companies) to facilitate our Services. These providers have access to your information only to perform tasks on our behalf.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect the rights, property, or safety of Screen Pigeon, our users, or others.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction.

4. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request details about the personal information we have collected about you in the past 12 months.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (e.g., legal obligations).
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of your personal information, if applicable.
  • Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@screenpigeon.io.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Personalize Your Experience: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze Website Performance: Monitor website traffic and performance for improvements.
  • Marketing: Provide relevant content and advertising based on your browsing behavior.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no security measure is completely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Information: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or Update Your Information: Update your personal information if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
  • Delete Your Information: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing Communications: Unsubscribe from promotional emails by following the instructions in the email.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When we no longer need your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.
